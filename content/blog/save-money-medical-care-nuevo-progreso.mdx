## The Big Picture: Why Medical Care in Nuevo Progreso Costs Less

Before we get into specific prices, it helps to understand *why* the savings are so dramatic:

- **Lower operating costs.** Clinic rent, staff salaries, and insurance in Mexico are a fraction of U.S. costs. Those savings get passed directly to patients.
- **No middlemen.** In the U.S., you're paying for hospital administration layers, insurance company overhead, and pharmaceutical markups. In Nuevo Progreso, the pricing structure is simpler.
- **Competition.** There are hundreds of clinics packed into a few blocks of Nuevo Progreso. That kind of competition keeps prices honest.
- **Direct-to-patient pricing.** No insurance negotiations, no "chargemaster" games. What you see is what you pay.

The result? The same quality materials, often the same training (many Nuevo Progreso dentists studied in the U.S.), at a price that actually makes sense.

### What About Quality?

This is the question everyone asks, and it deserves an honest answer.

Nuevo Progreso has been a dental destination for over 30 years. The best clinics use the same implant brands (Straumann, Nobel Biocare, Zimmer), the same CAD/CAM crown technology, and the same sterilization standards you'd find in a U.S. office.

The difference isn't quality — it's the business model. A dentist in Nuevo Progreso can do 8 to 12 crown preps a day because demand is high and overhead is low. Volume and lower costs, not corner-cutting, explain the price difference.

**How to verify quality:**

### Important Notes About Pharmacy Purchases

- **Bring your prescription.** Mexican pharmacies often sell medications over the counter that require prescriptions in the U.S., but having your prescription documentation helps — especially when re-entering the U.S.
- **Stick to licensed pharmacies.** Nuevo Progreso has legitimate pharmacies with licensed pharmacists. Avoid street vendors selling medications.
- **Quantity for personal use is fine.** U.S. customs generally allows you to bring back a 90-day supply of medications for personal use. Keep medications in original packaging.
- **Not everything is cheaper.** Some newer, brand-name medications may not have dramatic price differences. Generic medications show the biggest savings.

## How to Plan Your Medical Trip to Nuevo Progreso

Don't just show up and pick the first clinic you see. Use a vetting platform like ClearCross Progreso to:

- Read verified patient reviews

### Step 2: Schedule Your Appointment
Most clinics prefer appointments, especially for dental work. Contact your chosen clinic:
- By phone or WhatsApp (most respond quickly)
- Confirm the procedure, estimated cost, and timing
- Ask whether they need X-rays or records from your U.S. dentist
- Request a written treatment plan before you arrive

### Step 3: Prepare Your Documents
Bring:
- Valid passport or passport card
- Prescription documentation for any medications
- Insurance card (some procedures can be submitted for partial reimbursement)
- Cash or cards (most clinics accept both, but cash sometimes gets a small discount)

### Step 4: Cross the Border
The Progreso–Nuevo Progreso bridge is one of the easiest border crossings in Texas. Cross early on a weekday morning for the shortest lines. The walk from the parking lot to most clinics takes 10 to 15 minutes.

### Step 5: Get Your Treatment and Head Home
Most dental and medical appointments take 1 to 3 hours. After your procedure:
- Get written documentation of everything done
- Ask for X-rays on a CD or digital copies
- Confirm any follow-up appointments
- Get the clinic's contact information for post-care questions
