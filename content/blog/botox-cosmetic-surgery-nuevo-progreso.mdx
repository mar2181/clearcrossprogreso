Botox is one of the most popular cosmetic procedures, and the price difference between the US and Mexico is substantial.

- Per unit: $10-15
- Typical forehead treatment (20 units): $200-300
- Crow's feet (20 units): $200-300
- Full face (60 units): $600-900
- Total annual maintenance: $2,400-3,600

**In Nuevo Progreso:**
- Per unit: $2-4

- Crow's feet (20 units): $40-80

**Savings: 70-80% per treatment**

### How Botox Works
Botox (botulinum toxin) temporarily paralyzes facial muscles, reducing the appearance of wrinkles. Results appear gradually over 3-7 days and peak at 2 weeks. Effects last 3-4 months, requiring repeat treatments.

### Treatment Areas
- **Forehead lines**: 15-20 units
- **Crow's feet**: 12-24 units (both sides)
- **Frown lines** (between eyebrows): 20-25 units
- **Bunny lines** (nose): 8-12 units
- **Lip flip**: 4-8 units
- **Full face package**: 50-70 units

### Botox Safety
Botox is FDA-approved and safe when administered by qualified practitioners. Mexican cosmetic doctors use the same authentic Botox (or Xeomin, Dysport) as US practitioners. Counterfeit versions are extremely rare in reputable clinics.

### Types of Fillers Available
- **Hyaluronic acid** (Juvéderm, Restylane): Most popular, natural results, reversible if needed

- **Autologous fat**: Your own fat harvested and injected

### Filler Longevity

- **Poly-L-lactic acid**: 24+ months
- **Fat transfer**: Can be permanent

## Finding Cosmetic Providers

**Credentials:**
- Board certification (Mexican equivalent or international)
- Dermatology or plastic surgery training
- Years of experience (at least 5-10 years)
- Before-and-after portfolio

**Facility Quality:**
- Modern equipment
- Sterile, professional environment
- Proper anesthesia capabilities (if needed)
- Emergency protocols

**Communication:**
- English-speaking staff
- Clear explanation of procedures
- Honest about realistic results
- Answers your questions thoroughly

### Red Flags
- Unwilling to show before-and-afters
- Guaranteeing specific results
- Pushing you into procedures you're unsure about
- Unclear pricing
- Poor facility cleanliness
- Negative reviews about results

## Safety Considerations

### Choosing Reputable Providers
The risk of complications increases dramatically with inexperienced or under-qualified providers. Choose established clinics with strong reviews and documented experience.

### Common Side Effects
Most cosmetic procedures have minimal side effects:
- **Botox**: Bruising, swelling, temporary drooping (rare)
- **Fillers**: Bruising, swelling, lumps (usually resolve)
- **Chemical peels**: Temporary redness and peeling
- **Laser**: Temporary redness, sensitivity

Serious complications are rare with qualified providers using proper techniques.

### Avoiding Complications
- Choose experienced providers
- Follow pre- and post-treatment instructions
- Start with conservative doses
- Choose non-invasive options first (Botox, fillers) before surgery
- Ask about qualifications and experience
- Get everything in writing

## Recovery and Results Timeline

### Fillers

- **Recovery**: 3-7 days downtime, can be social
- **Results**: Skin improves over weeks

### Laser Treatments
- **Recovery**: 1-2 weeks downtime
- **Results**: Progressive improvement over weeks/months
- **Duration**: Ongoing improvement, may need touch-ups

### Surgical Procedures (Facelift, Liposuction, etc.)
- **Recovery**: 1-2 weeks visible recovery, 2-3 months full recovery

## Planning Your Cosmetic Trip

### Timing
Most cosmetic procedures can be combined with dental appointments since they're often done by different providers. Plan:
- **Morning**: Dental appointments
- **Afternoon**: Cosmetic treatments or shopping

### Post-Care Instructions
Get clear written instructions for post-care:
- What to avoid
- How to care for treated areas
- When to expect results
- When to return for touch-ups
- Emergency contact information

### Follow-Up Care
Some procedures require follow-ups:
- **Botox/Fillers**: Possible touch-ups at 2 weeks
- **Veneers**: Final adjustment after placement
- **Laser**: Multiple sessions for optimal results
- **Surgery**: Suture removal, scar care, follow-ups
